About the Camp

DOMINATE THE
OFF-SEASON

OFFSZN Hockey is an intensive 8-week off-season training program designed for competitive youth, AA, AAA, and high school prep players who are serious about taking their game to the next level.

Our program builds power, speed, endurance, and skill so athletes return to their teams stronger, faster, and more confident than ever before.

✓
Competitive youth, AA, AAA & high school prep players
✓
2 hours per session — 1 hour on-ice + 1 hour off-ice
✓
Monday, Tuesday & Thursday each training week
✓
Built-in 4th of July week break — no charge
✓
Limited spots for individualized coaching attention
Program Structure
